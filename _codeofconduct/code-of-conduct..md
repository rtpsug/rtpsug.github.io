<div class="pg-body">
  <div class="pg-container">
    <div class="pg-card">

      <div class="pg-card-title">Community Code of Conduct</div>
      <p class="pg-card-intro">Our community is dedicated to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members. The following guidelines ensure a respectful, safe, and collaborative space for everyone involved.</p>

      <div class="pg-section">
        <div class="pg-section-title">1. Respectful Behavior</div>
        <p class="pg-section-text">Treat all members with respect and kindness, regardless of differences in opinion, background, race, gender, sexual orientation, abilities, or beliefs.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="pg-section">
        <div class="pg-section-title">2. Inclusive Environment</div>
        <p class="pg-section-text">Foster an inclusive atmosphere by being open-minded, welcoming, and supportive of diverse perspectives. Encourage participation from everyone and avoid discriminatory or exclusionary language or actions.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="pg-section">
        <div class="pg-section-title">3. Constructive Communication</div>
        <p class="pg-section-text">Engage in constructive and considerate discussions. Avoid inflammatory or derogatory remarks, personal attacks, harassment, or any form of unwelcome behavior.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="pg-section">
        <div class="pg-section-title">4. Appreciation of Diversity</div>
        <p class="pg-section-text">Embrace the diversity of experiences and viewpoints within our community. Celebrate the richness that different backgrounds bring to our discussions and interactions.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="pg-section">
        <div class="pg-section-title">5. Collaboration and Learning</div>
        <p class="pg-section-text">Encourage collaboration, knowledge sharing, and learning from one another. Offer guidance and support to fellow members, fostering an environment conducive to growth and development.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="pg-section">
        <div class="pg-section-title">6. Respect Privacy</div>
        <p class="pg-section-text">Respect the privacy and confidentiality of other members. Refrain from sharing personal information without consent and be mindful of the sensitivity of certain topics.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="pg-section">
        <div class="pg-section-title">7. Responsible Use of Resources</div>
        <p class="pg-section-text">Use community resources responsibly and ethically. Avoid spamming, trolling, or engaging in any activities that could disrupt or harm the community's functionality or purpose.</p>
      </div>

      <hr class="pg-rule">

      <div class="pg-enforcement">
        <div class="pg-enforcement-title">Enforcement</div>
        <p class="pg-enforcement-text">Violations of this code of conduct may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or removal from the community, depending on the severity and repetition of the behavior. Community moderators hold the authority to enforce these guidelines.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="pg-enforcement" style="margin-top: 10px;">
        <div class="pg-enforcement-title">Reporting Incidents</div>
        <p class="pg-enforcement-text">If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code of conduct, please report it to a moderator or administrator. All reports will be taken seriously and handled promptly and confidentially.</p>
      </div>

      <hr class="pg-rule">

      <p class="pg-section-text" style="color: var(--pg-gray-500); font-style: italic;">By being a part of this community, you agree to abide by this code of conduct and contribute to creating a positive and welcoming environment for all members.</p>

    </div>
