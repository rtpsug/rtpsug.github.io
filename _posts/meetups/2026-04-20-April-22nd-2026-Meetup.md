Ever wonder how to wrangle AWS resources from the comfort of your PowerShell
console? Join us as we explore AWS Tools for PowerShell and see how they can
make your cloud management tasks easier, faster, and far more repeatable.

<!--more-->

## 📘 Meeting Overview:

In this hands-on session, we’ll start from the ground up by setting up the AWS
CLI, installing the necessary modules, and walking through the different ways to
authenticate securely. From there, we’ll show you how to seamlessly move between
accounts and efficiently search for the resources you need without the usual
friction.

Once the foundation is in place, we’ll dive into real-world scenarios. You’ll
see practical examples of spinning up EC2 instances, managing S3 buckets,
creating IAM users, invoking Lambda functions, and querying CloudWatch logs—all
from PowerShell scripts you can take home and use immediately.

We’ll keep things practical and engaging with live demos that highlight both
quick wins and the inevitable “gotchas.” Whether you’re just getting started
with AWS automation or looking to level up your scripting game, you’ll walk away
with working code examples, best practices, and tips for troubleshooting when
things don’t go quite as planned.

📰 Extra credit: Bring your questions, your war stories, and your curiosity.
This is a space for PowerShell enthusiasts to learn, share, and build together.
